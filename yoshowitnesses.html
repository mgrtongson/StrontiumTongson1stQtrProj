Have you encountered a conversation of anything trending in the Philippines such as the inflation of gas prices in the country and the shortage of beep cards for public transportation in Metro Manila? These things you hear are based on articles that have been reported through witnesses. Witnesses are, to put simply, observing a situation happening. In fact, seeing a crumb on the floor being plagued by ants is already considered a witness. Even noticing your deadlines for classroom requirements, or seeing your family talking about other witnesses, talk about witnessception!

Anyway, talking about witnesses, let's dive into how they turn into articles you read online.

Sparking witnesses committed in the Philippines

Let's use a sample well-known topic to demonstrate the fundamentals of witnesses to keep you from boredom. Say for example let's talk about how positively or negatively impactful Marcos Sr.'s administration was towards the Philippines as a people and as a nation.

During this time, much time was dedicated to expanding the Philippine road network with the money for transportation given as well as for certain prominent buildings built for us today.

At the same time this happened, however, multiple abuses towards working Filipinos intending to serve the communities occurred because of interference with the terribly menacing armies created. Not only that, but the economy was taking a huge toll regarding the inflation of the peso as well as the unemployment rate from mid-1960s until 1986.

Effects on said subject on the Philippines as a Nation

As you can tell from the two situations, when one reads both of them, they would notice that on one hand it seems as if Marcos Sr.'s administration has indeed contributed a lot of infrastructure during his time, but as a result of stolen wealth and abusing human life, the economy has gone a downward spiral with a large inflation spike occurring towards the end of his regime at almost P20 to 1 dollar as opposed to originally ~P4 to 1 dollar at the start of his first term.

From a Political Interpretation to a Controversial Clash

These two impacts of the administration of Marcos Sr., with a bit more emphasis on the Martial Law era, have given rise to two major groups who both speak out loud Macoy's Name, both Pro and Anti, who still do to this day due to how much his administration has affected the Philippines and its economy.
