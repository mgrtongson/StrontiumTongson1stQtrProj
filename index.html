Welcome to the home of Maliwagnag!


The internet is a very strange place indeed, it is filled with mysterious and yet potentially malicious content, causing a tough dillemma on a person's decisions on what to trust.


Trust in short is crucial for any internet user because it may impact their experiences on connecting with others and it could strengthen or weaken the ability for one to do so here.


Millions of accounts every year unfortunately get trapped into several internet holes such as trusting news resources that turn out to be hoaxes. It is now very easy for misinformation to spread due to put simply how simple of a couple of clicks could bring about to so many people to get tricked. In short, misinformation can lead to people dealing with other social issues incorrectly.


According to Statista.com, 26% of people in the United States are aware of the presence of fake news in society today, and because of that, 38.8% of them feel more obliged to pay attention to the articles that they read.


Fortunately, you are just in the right place for any such sticky situation: The home of the Maliwagnag family.


Meet each of the 5 members of this family who are of expertise when it comes to analyzing internet resources and how reliable or scamming each one found can turn out.

What are you waiting for? Click any of their icons to bring a revolutionary change to your trust ability and spread proper awareness of different social issues, including misinformation as a global issue itself.

Colin is a 21-year-old who is very interested in the origin of things, especially those of which we seem to rely on our everyday lives, including platforms like Facebook, Twitter, Messenger, Discord, as well as the origin and foundations of the internet.


Yosho is a 19-year-old who is very observant of what is happening around the environment, from humans to natural calamities. They like to ensure accurate information is being interpreted from whoever is in the witness of the situation. Otherwise, they will investigate the case of the truth as they always strive for it.


Gabiley is a 17-year-old that sticks to her social media such as Facebook and Twitter looking up events that are currently happening and how each one trending affects everybody, from each user and their personal life to a global trending event such as the still-ongoing COVID-19 pandemic


Fendworth is a 51-year-old who is the most experienced in the family in analyzing the internet who digs up the roots of every major rumor going around that ends up either getting busted or proven using evidence such as properly-researched statistics/data and in-person interviews.


Lastly, Kelly is a 53-year-old who is the mother of the family and just like other moms, she always lectures her children to be careful in browsing what is going on in the internet since possible misinformation traps can be tempting with no giveaway signs of suspicions. But Kelly is also a teacher, so she is capable of teaching us to practice good netiquette when it comes to collecting information, too.
